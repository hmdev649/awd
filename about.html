About Us

Welcome to the definitive online directory for American watchmakers and watch brands. The directory exists to spotlight the passion, creativity, and ingenuity fueling horology across the United States.

Our mission is simple: to increase visibility for American watchmakers, foster collaboration, and help drive a new golden era for domestic watchmaking. From independent artisans crafting one-of-a-kind pieces to established brands rooted in tradition, we bring together the full spectrum of talent and innovation in one place.

Visibility & Marketing

We help watchmakers reach new audiences and grow their brand through enhanced online exposure.

Customer Engagement & Lead Generation

Whether you're a collector, enthusiast, or first-time buyer, our directory makes it easy to discover and connect with American watchmakers that align with your interests.

Supporting Recruitment & Supply Chains

We help watchmakers find skilled talent and reliable parts and accessories suppliers to strengthen the backbone of American horology.
